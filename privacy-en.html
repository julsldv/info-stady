Privacy policy

Last update: February 10, 2025

1. Data controller

The data controller for personal data is Stady. For any questions regarding your personal data, you can contact us at: appstady@gmail.com.

2. Data collected

We collect the following data in connection with your use of the Stady application:

  • Account data: email address, nickname, first name, last name, profile photo, profile visibility setting (public or private).
  • Content data: the matches you create (teams, scores, date, venue, sport, competition, match photos, comments).
  • Technical data: login identifier (Firebase Authentication), data required for the application to function (hosting, authentication, storage).

3. Purposes and legal basis

Your data is processed for: creating and managing your account; providing the service (recording and displaying your matches, venues); personalising the application (profile, visibility); and complying with our legal obligations.

The legal basis is performance of the contract (use of the service) and, where applicable, your consent (for example for profile visibility or use of certain features).

4. Retention period

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may at any time request deletion of your account and associated data via the "Delete my account" function in the application or by contacting us. Some data may be retained for the periods required by law (accounting obligations, disputes).

5. Recipients and processors

Your data is hosted and processed via Firebase (Google) services: authentication (Firebase Auth), database (Firestore), file storage (Firebase Storage). These processors act on our instructions and in accordance with confidentiality and security commitments. You can consult Google's privacy policies for more information.

6. Transfers outside the European Union

The servers of the providers we use (including Google) may be located in countries outside the European Union. In such cases, appropriate safeguards are in place (EU standard contractual clauses or adequacy decisions) to protect your data in accordance with European law.

7. Your rights (GDPR)

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights: right of access to your data; right to rectification; right to erasure ("right to be forgotten"); right to data portability; right to object to processing; right to restriction of processing; right to withdraw your consent at any time. You may exercise these rights by contacting us at the address given in section 1. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority (e.g. CNIL in France: www.cnil.fr).

8. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, destruction or alteration. Authentication and communications are secured (HTTPS, Firebase services).

9. Changes

We may update this privacy policy. The last update date is shown at the top of the document. In the event of a substantial change, we will inform you, for example via a notice in the application or by email.

10. Contact us

For any questions about this policy or to exercise your rights, contact us at: appstady@gmail.com.
